    • Toast
    Toast display messages, important notifications or a response of a particular action for a certain interval of time usually 2-3 seconds.
    Solid Toast
    This toast displays response with solid background. This can also be used with different colors like primary, secondary, etc
    Kakashi Hatake, success 
                        
                        <button class="btn primary" data-toast>Toast</button>
                        <div class="toast success">
                            Kakashi Hatake, success
                            <button class="btn-close"></button>
                        </div>
    Outline Toast
    This toast displays response with outline color.
    Kakashi Hatake, success
